# Hero video

Drop a looping background video here named `hero-loop.mp4` and the hero will
play it automatically (duotoned black/gold by a CSS filter, dimmed under a
scrim for text contrast).

Guidelines (per the FLH spec):
- Abstract or campus footage, 15–30s seamless loop, no audio
- 1920×1080, H.264, keep it under ~6 MB (compress hard; it's a background)
- The page works fine without it — it falls back to the gold particle field.
